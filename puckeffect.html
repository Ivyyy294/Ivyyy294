PUCK Effect

A sci-fi reimagining of A Midsummer Night's Dream, "PUCK Effect" is a chat simulator where players are stuck in a spaceship room, exploring relationships between the four passengers. It’s a 3D first-person game that begins as a romance but quickly turns dark as the ship's AI interferes.
